digital economy & technology summit 2022

The world digital economy & technology summit is a high-level forum bringing together key stakeholders in the digital economy to discuss key trends, strategic challenges, major issues and risks impacting on our digital future.

2022.1.2(Mon) ~ 3(Tue)

@ Sule Shangri-La Hotel, Yangon

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening.

Project

Appreciate various projects applying WDET license of alumni, organized from Shangri-La.

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

Workshop

Try creating your own work using open source license rather than just watching.

Ignite

Get oppotunities to network with WDET affiliates around the world, also after the summit.
